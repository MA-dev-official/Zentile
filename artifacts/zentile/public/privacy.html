Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Short version: Zentile does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The game runs entirely in your browser and works offline after the first load.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Zentile has no user accounts, no sign-up form, no analytics scripts, and no advertising trackers.

2. Local Storage

Zentile saves your best scores (number of moves and completion time per board size) in your browser's localStorage. This data:

  • Stays on your device only.
  • Is never sent to any server.
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data for this site.

3. Cookies

Zentile does not set any cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

The Zentile application does not load any third-party scripts, fonts, images, or trackers. All assets are served from the same origin as the game.

If the site is hosted on a third-party platform (for example, Vercel), that platform may collect basic, anonymized request logs (such as IP address and user-agent) for security and operational purposes. Please refer to your hosting provider's privacy policy for details.

5. Children's Privacy

Zentile is appropriate for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will appear on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the site operator through the channel they have provided to you.
